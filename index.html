About Us

ANGIES BEAUTY PALACE is your premier destination for beauty and empowerment in our local community. We are a proud Black-owned salon that specializes in makeup artistry, catering to a diverse clientele with a focus on enhancing natural beauty and celebrating individual uniqueness. Founded in March 2024,Our was born out of a passion for beauty and a desire to create a welcoming space where everyone can feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated. Our mission is to offer exceptional makeup services while promoting self-love and confidence. We believe that beauty is not one-size-fits-all, and our goal is to highlight the unique features of every client who walks through our doors. We are dedicated to providing a safe and inclusive environment where everyone feels respected and valued.
